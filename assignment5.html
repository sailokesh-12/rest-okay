Momos

Momos are popular dumplings originating from Tibet and enjoyed in Nepal, India, Bhutan, and East Asia. They consist of a thin dough wrapper filled with vegetables, meat, cheese, or sweet fillings. Cooked through steaming or frying, momos are served with dipping sauces and can be enjoyed as a snack, appetizer, or main course.

Types of Momos

  • Vegetable Momos

  • Cheese Momos

  • Spinach Momos

  • Chocolate Momos

  • Paneer Momos

  • Chicken Momos

  • Mutton Momos
